Risotto

Description

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup mushroom mix (shitakes, creminis, morel)
  • 1 Cup Arborio rice
  • 1 litre Vegetable Stock
  • 1 lemon
  • olive oil
  • few tabs of butter
  • freshly grated parmesean cheese
  • chives or green onion
  • 2 small to medium shallots
  • 1 bottle of dry white wine

Steps

  1. Warm stock with lid until boiling and then turn off heat.
  2. Thinly slice mushrooms and then cook on medium heat with oil for 3 minutes until tender. Remove from heat with liquid.
  3. Dice up shallots and fry with a small amount of butter until softened.
  4. Add the rice to the shallots with some oil. Move around to coat rice and cook for a few minutes until it takes on a golden colour.
  5. Add 1/4 Cup wine to deglaze and stir rice to incorporate. Pour the rest of the wine into a glass and enjoy while you cook.
  6. Slice up lemon and squeeze juice, enduring that no seeds fall into rice.
  7. Begin by adding half a ladle of the stock and continuously stir the rice until all the liquid is incorporated.
  8. Repeat the process of adding the stock, slowly building up to a full ladle. Be sure that all the liquid is incorporated into the rice before adding more.
  9. After all the stock is abosrded, the rice should be soft and creamy. Add at least a tablespoon of butter (or more if preferred), salt and pepper.
  10. After seasoning properly, add mushroom with its liquid and stir into stock and remove from heat. Grate parmesean and stir into risotto.
  11. Serve in bowls and then chop up some chives and add right before serving.
