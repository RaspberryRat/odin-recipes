Pizza

Description

Ingredients

Dough

  • 2 cups flours
  • 1 cup water
  • 3 teaspoons yeast
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 3 tablespoons onion powder

Toppings

  • ladle of tomato sauce
  • half a log of costco mozza
  • any other toppings you desire

Steps

  1. Add 1 Cup of warm water to the yeast and wait for 10 minutes
  2. Mix the flour, salt, onion powder and oil together
  3. Add the water and yeast to the flour and thoroughly mix dough with hands
  4. Cover with plastic wrap for at least 1 hour, 3+ hours is better
  5. While you wait, cut up mozza into chunks and cut up meat and vegetables to desired size
  6. 20 minutes before, pre-heat oven with a pizza stone to 375 F
  7. After dough has sufficiently risen, remove stone from oven and add a light covering of cornmeal. Then lightly dust dough with flour and roll out on a pre-warmed pizza stone
  8. Add sauce and evenly spread across dough, leaving some room around the edge for the crust
  9. Add cut up mozza evenly across sauce
  10. Add other toppings
  11. Cook for 30 minutes
  12. After removing from oven let cool for 3-5 minutes, preferably on a wire rack
