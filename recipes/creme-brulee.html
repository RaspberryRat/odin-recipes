Crème Brûlée

Description

Ingredients

  • 3 Cups whipping cream (heavy cream)
  • 5 large egg yolks
  • 150 grams of white granulated sugar

Steps

  1. crack eggs and drain whites, and put egg yolks into a mixing bowl.
  2. Add ONLY 100 grams of the sugar to the egg yolks and whisk until thoroughly mixed.
  3. Pour cream into a pot and heat on medium-high. As soon as cream begins to simmer, remove from heat.
  4. Pour 1/2 Cup of warmed cream into a measuring cup.
  5. Boil about 1 Litre of water and pre-heat oven to 325 F.
  6. Slowly (small dribble) pour the 1/2 Cup of cream into the eggs. Whisk continuously. If you don't pour slowly or whisk the whole time, eggs will scramble.
  7. After all the cream is poured into the egg yolk/sugar mix. Slowly pour the mixture into the rest of the warmed cream, whisking continuously.
  8. After the cream and egg yolk mixture is mixed, pour mixture to the top of a ramekin, leaving a small gap.
  9. Put the ramekins into baking dish, and add boiling water to dish, covering the ramekins 1 inch deep.
  10. Put the baking dish into the ovens and cook for 30-40 minutes. Start checking after 30 minutes. Edges should be firm while centre is slighly jiggly.
  11. After cooked, carefully remove from the water and let cool uncovered on a wire rack for at least one hour.
  12. After cooled, loosley cover and put in the fridge for at least 4 hours, and up to 4 days.
  13. Right before serving, remove from fridge and sprinkle a layer of sugar (ensure it fully covers surface). Then, use a torch to melt the sugar. Should quickly cool into a hard layer.
  14. Serve immediately.
